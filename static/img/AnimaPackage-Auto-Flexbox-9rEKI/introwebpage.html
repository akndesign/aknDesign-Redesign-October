I design, code and develop user interfaces. I use React, JavaScript, and dabble in Python.  I examine joy, play and discovery in my work.
Howdy! I’m Alexander.
Misfits is a new stacking game, primarily for adults. The game’s objective is to be first to stack ten blocks, without the tower falling.  No two stacks are the same. It’s nail-biting. Surprising. Delightful.
My client-based work has got the attention of Buzzfeed, the Globe and Mail, and Wallpaper Magazine.

My freelance clients include Christopher Wylie (Cambridge Analytica), Citymapper, and UsTwo.
